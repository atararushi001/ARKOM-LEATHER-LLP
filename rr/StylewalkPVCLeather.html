Urbane PVC Leather

Overview :

Vortex, the PVC footwear, shoes, leather sole lining fabric manufacturer in India offers specialized products for the footwear industry. We guarantee you a durable and functional StyleWalk synthetic leather range specifically meant for high use applications in shoe linings, insoles, and uppers. Our complete series of PVC leather for footwear and PVC leather for shoes are meant even for the healthcare and defense sectors.

At Vortex, we are dedicated to providing the avant-garde product to our customers. We have been rated the best among the artificial leather manufacturers in India because we deliver on quality and consistency. Our StyleWalk range of PVC leather for shoes meets all the industry specifications and technical standards. Our PVC leather for footwear almost looks like real leather and has a host of benefits. It is scratch-resistant and weather-durable. Extra caution is taken to manufacture these PVC leather for shoes so that it can sustain the harsh weather conditions ranging from below zero temperatures to the higher ones.

The StyleWalk range is available in varied textures and colors. Our PVC leather for shoes is fully customized from the design, texture, and pattern to the thickness and the material depending on the customer requirements. We being the largest PVC footwear fabric manufacturer in India, take special care to deliver the best to our customers.

Applications

Our StyleWalk range of PVC footwear fabric spans the entire footwear industry right from shoe uppers, shoe linings to the insoles.

  • Footwear- We manufacture PVC leather for footwear consisting of artificial leather fabric for shoe uppers, insoles, and shoe linings.
  • Cold Temperatures- Our manufactured PVC leather for shoes is suitable for extremely cold temperatures and can be used in snow, high altitude, trekking, mountaineering, and for defense forces.
  • Healthcare- We have special anti-bacterial synthetic leather in footwear fabric that is perfect for use in clinics and hospitals.
