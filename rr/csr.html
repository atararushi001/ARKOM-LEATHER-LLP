HR POLICATES PVT LTD

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

FOR A GOOD CAUSE

 We believe in “Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile”

Our policy is not only to do business. We firmly believe it is also our responsibility to contribute to the social well-being of our society, we therefore actively participate in the sports activities run by ‘JOON EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE TRUST’ which helps the local communities and poor people to get their children/youth trained in the field of sports.

At ARKOM, excellence isn't a destination

– We seek to make a positive impact by promoting rural sports and scouting the talent amongst the rural and far-flung areas of the country and training them to develop their skills to achieve excellence in their respective sports discipline.
– ARKOM's vision to promote sports activities is to improve the lives of children and youth to have a better future and eternal hope which may be a big support for the preparation of the country’s best athlete and also be a collective effort towards nation-building.
